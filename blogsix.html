I may have bought more plants” – Me to my disappointed boyfriend

22/11/2019
6.45pm

So, after two hours of work I thought it would be a good idea to stop for a wee break and take the dogs to the beach.
However, on the way to the beach, me and the flatmate stopped off at the warehouse, and we may have spent a large amount of time looking at plants. And god damnit I bought some more as well as a new pot.



Two lovely little ivy plants and a twin for Albert. They don’t have names yet but I’m sure over the next couple of days I will come up with some. The two ivy plants will go into a pot together with some supports to help them grow into a nice arch. The little pink one will go into the brand-new pot and id say he’ll fil it up very soon.

That now brings my total plant family up to 11!!!!!!
